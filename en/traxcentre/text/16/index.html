ZF Service

Truck Centre Ltd. has the official status of ZF Service Partner in Ukraine (Division of Trucks, Buses and trolleybuses).
Therefore Truck Centre Ltd. has complete sets of ZF technical documentation, ZF diagnostic equipment, ZF special tools, a test stand for ZF Ecomat gearboxes and qualified mechanics trained at ZF's factories.

We provide diagnosis, maintenance, warranty and post warranty repairs of the following ZF components:

- mechanical gearboxes ZF 16S150, 16S181, 16S220
- automatic transmission ZF AS Tronic, Ecomat
- ZF front axles
- ZF rear axles
- ZF steering box
- other ZF components.

Spare parts come straight from ZF's factories.
